A few baking tips..!

  1. Measure the ingredients correctly.
  2. Preheat your oven to the set temperature.
  3. Make use of room temperature ingredients.
  4. Make use of fresh baking soda/powder.
  5. Don't overmix the batter.
  6. Rotate the pans for even baking in the oven.
